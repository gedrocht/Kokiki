# Payroll Basics

Payroll is the process of turning worked time and company rules into actual pay.

## Important beginner terms

- **Gross pay**: money earned before deductions.
- **Net pay**: money left after deductions.
- **Overtime pay**: extra pay for overtime hours.
- **Benefit deduction**: money removed for benefits such as insurance.
- **Tax withholding**: money held back for taxes.

## In this repository

The COBOL engine is responsible for the arithmetic. The Spring Boot client is
responsible for validation, orchestration, and presentation.
